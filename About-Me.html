About Me

Driven, Ready to Learn

Hi there! I’m Andrew Estrada, a recent Mechanical Engineering graduate from Northern Arizona University. My passion for engineering started early on, fueled by a curiosity to understand how things work and a desire to create. Growing up, I was always taking things apart and finding ways to improve them, which naturally led me to pursue a career in engineering.

Today, I’m channeling that passion into projects like designing and building components for a race car as part of my work with the Formula SAE team, as well as high power rocketry. I’m excited to continue exploring the limitless possibilities within engineering and to share my journey with you. Feel free to check out my resume to learn more!
